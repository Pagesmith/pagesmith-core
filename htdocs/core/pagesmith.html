What is Pagesmith?

Pagesmith is the Institute's internal site management solution, written in a mixture of perl, javascript and CSS, and is built around Apache 2 with mod_perl and subversion to handle web-requests and site management respectively.

The solution is the culmination of over ten years of web-development by the core team, bringing exprience of managing many complex and diverse web-sites.

Pagesmith is an open source software project developed at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute - copyright ©2009-2012 Genome Research Limited
